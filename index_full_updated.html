About Adyts

Adyts started within the digital transformation domain providing core consulting services from the inception to the implementation project stages. It is predominantly specialized in helping organizations realize true value through ERP and Blockchain implementations. The company later expanded its operations into the Financial Instrument Trading segment with a strong focus on automation.

Company Structure

  • Finance and HR
  • Digital Transformation Operation
  • FinTech Trading Operation
  • Dev-Op / Offshore

Digital Transformation Services

  • Strategy definition supporting organizations to map long-term goals and enhance ROI on digital investment.
  • Define and develop sustainable architecture to help realize cost-optimized operation while adapting to ongoing technology disruption.
  • Effective project management approach with strong teams delivering outcomes on time and within budget.
  • In-house and offshore-based teams to build, test, and support implemented projects.
  • Ongoing operational review to realize full value potential and explore digital-based opportunities such as blockchain.

Financial Instrument Trading

  • In-house trading arm investing in financial instruments predominantly in the US market.
  • Local and offshore teams performing market analysis to identify trade opportunities.
  • Pooled investment in risk-managed portfolios.
  • Strong risk management approaches to maximize return.
  • Development of various projects to enhance trading strategies.
  • Ongoing development of an automated trading tool to be launched in the consumer market.
