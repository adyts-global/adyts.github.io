About Adyts

Adyts started within the digital transformation domain providing core consulting services from the inception to the implementation project stages. It is predominantly specialized in helping organizations realize true value through ERP and Blockchain implementations. The company later expanded its operation in the Financial Instrument Trading segment with strong focus on automation.

Company Structure

  • Finance and HR
  • Digital Transformation Operation
  • FinTech Trading Operation
  • Dev-Op / Offshore

Digital Transformation Services

  • Strategy definition supporting organization map long term goals to enhance the ROI on digital investment.
  • Define and develop sustainable architecture to help realise cost optimized operation while keeping up with constant technology disruption.
  • Effective Project Management approach with strong teams delivering outcomes on time and within budget.
  • In-house and offshore based teams to build, test and support implemented projects.
  • On-going operational review to realise full value potential of investment and explore digital based market opportunities such as blockchain.

Financial Instrument Trading

  • In-house trading arm of Adyts investing on financial instruments predominantly in the US market.
  • Local and offshore team performing market analysis identifying trade opportunities.
  • Pooled investment on risk-managed portfolios.
  • Strong risk management approaches to maximise return.
  • Development of various projects to enhance trading strategies.
  • On-going development of an automated trading tool to be launched in the consumer market.
